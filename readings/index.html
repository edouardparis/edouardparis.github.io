Here, I keep updated a list of book in order to keep me motivated. This list, started in 2017 follows no topic or particular guideline per year. Books are chosen by my curiosity and the recommendations of my friends.

Computer science and programming manuals are not included. I mostly cherry pick information from them.

If the title is translated in French: I read the French translation.
